40 Below

40 Below is a temperature app built for people, like me, who have friends, family, and coworkers that cross international borders. One of the most fundamental pieces of small talk we all engage in is, "how's the weather?"

I don't know about you, but telling my Canadian friends, "it's lovely! 76º and sunny!" doesn't mean much to them. This is where 40 Below comes in. With a quick check of the app I can tell them, "it's lovely! 24ºC and sunny!" That's the power of 40 Below: it does one thing and it does it really well.

Why it exists

One clear answer to one very common question

OK technically it does a couple more things than just that, but it does them well, too. 40 Below also includes a 10-hour forecast so you can see what's coming up in your day, and it also gives you the ability to share a beautifully rendered image with people.

What 40 Below does

Local weather, reduced to what matters most

Live Temperature in F and C

See Fahrenheit and Celsius side by side at all times, including feels-like values.

Hourly Forecast Panel

On iOS, swipe up to reveal the next 10 hours with temperature and feels-like context.

Location-Aware by Default

Uses your current location to show local conditions, with clear permission handling.

Sharing

Generate branded share images with optional location removal for privacy.

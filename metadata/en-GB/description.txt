40 Below was designed and built around one central purpose: to show you the current outdoor temperature in both Fahrenheit and Celsius. Many of us have friends, family and colleagues who live in other countries, and even the simplest chat about the weather can become awkward when those relationships cross the F/C divide. 40 Below cuts through all of that. With its prominent temperature reading front and centre, you're always only a moment away from being able to tell someone what the weather is like in the temperature scale they will understand.

International teams, travellers, expats and mixed-unit households alike will all find this handy little weather app useful.

Additional thoughtful features:
- See the “feels like” temperature in both °F and °C
- Check the temperature forecast for the coming hours so you can plan your day
- Share a beautifully rendered temperature card with or without your location

40 Below uses your location to show local conditions. You can control location access at any time in Settings.
